Our Policy on Refunds

We understand that you may be hesitant to spend money without fully knowing what you're in for, so leave that to us. We promise that this trip will be the pinnacle of your life, the best you will ever do, and we garuntee that if its not, a 100% full refund. We dont want you to worry about if you'll have fun, we know you will, so to make sure you can feel relaxed the entire time, we'll give you a refund if it doesnt meet your expectations.

 


 


 


 

Transportation On a Free Dive?

While we promise you'll be allowed to freedive to the mariana trench, the trench itself is 125 miles away from land. Because of this, we offer transportation included in your packages to ensure you don't have to worry about it. Depending on the package you buy, we can give you some of the best luxories in the world. Not only that, we'll give you top notch hospitality at the nearest country, so you're wait time to dive is unhindered and stress free.
